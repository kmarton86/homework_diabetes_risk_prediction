diabetes dataset:

Each row = one patient
442 patients total

['age', 'sex', 'bmi', 'bp', 's1', 's2', 's3', 's4', 's5', 's6']
These are features (inputs).
Important ones:
age → age of patient
sex → gender
bmi → body mass index (very important for diabetes)
bp → blood pressure

Target:

It represents:
how severe the diabetes disease is after one year

low value → mild / better condition
high value → worse condition

Example:
75   → low severity
150  → medium
300  → high severity

if target > 150 → Endangered
else → Not Endangered
